iPad

The ultimate investment solution for your ipad

Staying ahead with global trends and technical advancements, DirectFnm is proud to introduce the latest addition to our Product Suite-DirectFn ipad.

  • Live Market data at your finger tips
  • Latest Time & Sales
  • Intraday and historical charts with analytics
  • Latest News &Announcements with multi market coverage
Download

DFN iPad unleashes the power of DirectFN™ on your iPad. With this application, you have access to real-time price data, charts, news and announcements to keep you up-to-date with market activities. You can create personalized watch lists to keep track of your favorite symbols in multiple exchanges and view the market summary, top stocks information, market depth and announcements of subscribed exchanges.

Key Features:
DirectFN™ gives you an experience on your iPhone which is both user-friendly and secure. Simply log in to the application and the information that you require will be there at your fingertips.
  • Add multiple Watch Lists to keep track of your favorite stocks from regional and global markets.
  • View Top Stocks information for a number of markets, including the Top Gainers, Top Losers and Most-Traded stocks.
  • View Global Market Summary including Currencies, Commodities and World Indices.
  • View Market Summary for selected exchanges.
  • View Detail Quote for symbols, giving you a snapshot of all important information about the symbol.
  • View Market Depth information by Price and by Order.
  • Moving Ticker with configurable options.
  • View Real-time Announcements as released by the exchanges.
  • View latest News from various news providers.
  • Set Alerts to your favourite stocks and get notified about the latest moves.
  • Analyze the performance of symbols using Chart option featured with technical indicators.
  • Language support to facilitate you with desired language.
  • Dual Themes to customize the application as preferred.
Notes:
In order to view real-time data in mobile application, please subscribe to DFN Pro 10/DirectFN Web main packages.
